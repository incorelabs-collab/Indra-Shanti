Indrashanti

Ambikapur offers an eclectic mix of Temples, heritage buildings and is a modern, vibrant centre for commerce and industry. Indra Shanti, Ambikapur welcomes you with its elegant, simplistic design and an iconic hospitality. It has 23 rooms, tastefully appointed in muted and earthy tones, combined with furniture and convenient amenities, are designed to make you feel at home.


Places to explore

Mainpat, in the Surguja District, is a major tourist area near Ambikapur. It is also known as the "Hill station of Surguja" and the "Shimla of Chhatisgarh".


Other major tourist attraction

  • Tiger Point Waterfall and a Buddha Temple
  • Ramgarh
  • Kudargarh in Surajpur District (Bagheshwari Devi Temple)
  • Bhaiyathan in Surajpur District (Patal Bhairav Mandir)
  • Dipadih (Ancient Sculptors)
  • Rakasganda
  • Sitabengara
  • Tattapani (hot spring)
  • Semarsot
